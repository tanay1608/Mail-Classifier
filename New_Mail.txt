"Ek Ruka Hua Faisla" directed by Sukhesh Mishra

A Hindi adaptation by Ranjit Kapur of the famous Teleplay '12 Angry
Men' by Reginald Rose.

Synopsis - The story of a jury holdout attempting to prevent a
miscarriage of justice by forcing his colleagues to reconsider the
evidence.Following the closing arguments in a murder trial, the 12
members of the jury must deliberate, with a guilty verdict meaning death
for the accused, a slum born teen. As the dozen members try to reach a
unanimous decision while sequestered in a room, the play reaches the
epitome of thrill.

Campus Junta is invited to be part of this high suspense drama thriller.
